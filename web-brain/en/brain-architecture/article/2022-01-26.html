Lesson #2 (video)
Dynamic database of body positions
 January 26, 2022  Dr. Zdenek Moravcik
 
 

In this video I explain what human brain needs to control movement of one part of your body towards another part of your body. I explain that no map of your body is needed but instead your brain maintains database of 3D positions covering all your body parts.

This database of 3D positions is being recalculated in real-time any time position of your body part is changing. Based on this dynamic database of 3D positions any human movement is being calculated.

Video explains that all your movements are nothing more than trigonometric calculations that your brain is doing.
