Information addressing in human brain
Brain does have an “address bus”!
 April 17, 2022  Dr. Zdenek Moravcik
 
 

Video brings a proof that human brain has information addressing capabilities similar to addressing in a silicone computer. No one really knows how this addressing hardware is working in detail but this video makes it clear that address bus in a computer and addressing hardware inside human brain are of similar concept. Video also proposes solution how such an addressing hardware should (must) work and makes it clear what is a difference between short-term and long-term memory in human brain.
